Recycle Waste, Then Learn.

Join the movement towards a cleaner planet! This is your companion in waste management and environmental education. Explore different countries, complete quests, and make a positive impact on the world while learning about waste and its effects on the environment.

Level-Based Quests

Explore different countries and embark on unique quests tailored to each location. Earn points and unlock achievements as you progress through levels.

Oxygen & Life Levels

Track the health of each country's environment with real-time oxygen and life levels. Monitor the impact of waste on air quality and ecosystem health.
